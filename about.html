Who We Are

Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute Design/Build/Fly is a fully student-led organization of 49 undergraduate and graduate engineers.

Advised by Dr. Jason Hicken, the team is organized into Engineering and Administration divisions. We take an aircraft from conceptual sizing and sensitivity analysis in Python GEKKO, through rigorous FEA and CFD simulations, all the way to physical manufacturing and test flights.

By bridging the gap between classroom theory and real-world execution, our members develop the critical technical and project management skills required to become the next generation of aerospace leaders.
