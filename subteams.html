Aerodynamics

The Aerodynamics subteam is responsible for airfoil selection, outer mold line definition, and comprehensive stability analysis. Utilizing industry-standard software such as XFLR5, Simcenter STAR-CCM+, and MATLAB, we predict flight envelopes and validate our models through rigorous $1/4$ scale wind tunnel testing in the RPI subsonic wind tunnel.

Structures

Our Structures team engineers the wing, fuselage, and empennage to withstand rigorous flight loads of up to 8g. We design exclusively in SolidWorks and validate our load paths using Simcenter NASTRAN FEA. We specialize in balancing advanced carbon fiber monocoque designs with lightweight, semi-monocoque wood build-ups to maximize our competition score.

Propulsion

The heartbeat of the aircraft. The Propulsion team selects and tests motors, propellers, and avionics to maximize thrust and endurance within competition power limits. We utilize eCalc software and our own in-house dynamic thrust testing rigs to optimize our electric propulsion systems and integrate complex flight telemetry.

Subsystems

Subsystems focuses purely on mission-specific mechanisms. Because the AIAA DBF missions change every year, this team must be highly adaptable. For the 2026 competition, their work focuses heavily on designing rapid-release, servo-actuated payload hatches and the highly complex deployable 190" banner stowage and release system.

Manufacturing

The Manufacturing team translates digital CAD into physical aircraft. Operating out of our dedicated lab space, we specialize in advanced composite fabrication, including Vacuum Assisted Resin Transfer Method (VARTM) carbon fiber layups. We also heavily utilize PA6-CF additive manufacturing (3D printing), precision laser-cutting, and UltraCote heat shrink film application.

Documentation

A great aircraft cannot win without a great engineering report. The Documentation team ensures rigorous technical recording of all engineering processes and design decisions throughout the year. We utilize LaTeX typesetting and professional technical writing standards to compile our comprehensive, highly-scored AIAA design reports.
