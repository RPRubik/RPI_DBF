Design Reports

Read our past Design Reports detailing our aircraft engineering, analysis, and fabrication processes.

Each year, the team is required to write a comprehensive design report as a part of the competition. These reports summarize the design, analysis, construction, and testing of our plane each year. They are written beginning in around mid-November, submitted in late February, and given a score out of 100 in early April by professional aerospace engineers who volunteer their time for the competition. Every engineering lead is heavily involved in writing the report, and it gives our members a chance to develop their professional technical writing before they enter the workforce or graduate school.
